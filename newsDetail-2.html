Simbrella announces launch in South-East Asia via microlending partnership with MPT Myanmar.

With over 24 million users, MPT (Myanmar Posts and Telecommunications) is the first and leading telecommunications company in Myanmar, providing both fixed and mobile telecommunication services to people and enterprises of the country.

Our airtime lending solution for MPT includes data analytics, credit scoring of customers, processing, disbursement, repayment, recovery and management of mobile balance loans. We are happy to support MPT to achieve their main goals – to make telecommunications services more accessible nationwide and to move Myanmar forward by continual improvement of product portfolio.
