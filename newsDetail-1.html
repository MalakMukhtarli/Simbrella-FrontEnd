Simbrella launches "Lajor" - digital micro-lending product for Orange Money mobile wallets in Sierra Leone.

Orange Sierra Leone is one of the largest mobile network providers of the country with customer base. Lajor – the loan product designed by Simbrella is aimed at providing small short-term loans to Orange Money Sierra Leone customers in order to be spent for any payments available in Orange Money system. Orange Money is an innovative, mobile phone-based payment system that allows customers to carry out banking operations and transactions in total security, requiring no bank account.

Simbrella and Orange Sierra Leone started their cooperation in 2011 with successful integration of SimKredit™ platform for provision of airtime advances/credits. Launch of mobile wallet digital loans has strengthened the strategic partnership between two companies.
